Admissions Procedure

The School Management reserves to itself the right to admit or refuse pupils seeking admission to the school without furnishing any reasons whatsoever. Similarly, the management decides when the question of retaining a student in school arises. (Every Student Studying in Std 1-8 is required to clock an attendance of 90% during the Academic Year)

PROCEDURE FOR SEEKING ADMISSION

1.       

STEP 1

Registration

Registering the candidature for the desired class either ‘Online’ (by visiting School Website) or Walk-In the Front Desk Office.

2.       

STEP 2

Entrance Exam

 Admission Officer to conduct an exam as per the age/class in which the admission seeking for.

3.       

STEP 3

Interview/Interaction Session with the Principal

If the child succeeds to clear the ‘Entrance Exam’, an interview/interaction session of the candidate with his/her parents with the Principal.

4.       

STEP 4

Payment of Admission Fee

Admission charges to be paid in Accounts Department.

5.       

STEP 5

Documentation

Submission of all the asked documents (mentioned in ‘Documents to be Submitted’) along with Admission Form in Records Department.

6.       

STEP 6

Generating Unique ID

 There on, an ‘Admission Number’ will be issued to the enrolled candidacy.
